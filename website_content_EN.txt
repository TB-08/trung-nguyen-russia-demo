Core Values
Our Guiding Principles
Creativity
We believe creativity is the only force that produces world-class products — ones that transcend ordinary limits.
Respect for Heritage
We take pride in and champion the authentic qualities of Buon Ma Thuot Robusta, making it a cultural ambassador for Vietnam.
Responsibility
Committed to making a positive contribution to communities, protecting the environment, and supporting farmers on the path to sustainable development.
Service
Placing customers and partners at the centre of everything we do, and constantly striving to deliver the finest coffee experience.
Our History
1996
2003
2012
2018
2026
1996
Trung Nguyen was founded in Buon Ma Thuot — the "Coffee Capital" — beginning its journey to win over markets with the distinctive flavour of roasted coffee.
2003
The G7 instant coffee was launched, marking a major turning point and rapidly capturing the domestic market before expanding internationally.
2012
Trung Nguyen Coffee became a trusted brand in over 60 countries, with a particularly strong following in Asia and Russia.
2018
Brand repositioning with a premium product ecosystem, centred on the philosophy of "Coffee as Energy — Coffee that Changes Lives."
2026
Strategic expansion across the Russian Federation with comprehensive coffee solutions for B2B, HoReCa, and individual consumers.

To become a global provider of cultural and coffee values — delivering awakened energy to every creative mind in the world.
Grounded in a deep understanding of and pride in Vietnam's Robusta heritage, we aspire to set a new standard in how coffee is experienced: not merely a beverage, but the spirit of entrepreneurship and creation.
"Building a unified human community through the language of coffee."

Coffee Heritage
The Story of
Vietnamese Coffee
From the land of Buon Ma Thuot, rich Robusta beans pass through the art of roasting to become a cup of coffee full of energy and character.
Buon Ma Thuot · Vietnam
1996
Origins
Buon Ma Thuot —
The Land of Bold Coffee
In the story of Vietnamese coffee, Buon Ma Thuot stands as a landmark of raw material, culture, and national pride. It evokes images of red earth, highland climate, and coffee beans with a distinctly pronounced character.
For the international palate, this origin offers a memorable introduction: Vietnamese coffee means strength, depth, and a ritual all its own.

Two styles, two distinct experiences — the Trung Nguyen Legend range celebrates the body and depth that Vietnamese coffee is known for.

Coffee Tao
Coffee Tao is the brand's interpretation of coffee as a cultural experience: mindful enjoyment that awakens focus and connects people to the origins of flavour.
From the quick convenience of G7 to a phin-filtered pour, consumers choose the ritual that fits their moment — while sharing a common thread: bold taste, the image of Vietnam, and a spirit of confidence.
